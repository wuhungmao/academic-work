About this course

Difficulty: 8/10

Grade: 81/100

Reflection:

I took this course with professor Andrew petersen. Similar to CSC258, he gives fast paced lecture. I would recommended to read textbook and study course material prior to going to his lecture becuase he explains a lot of stuff in 2 hours every week. Any lack of relevant knowledge might result in confusion during his lecture. The course content takes some time to grasp and debugging multiple threads is not only a pain in the ass but also very time consuming. Each assignment requires a ideally 5+ pages analysis report and heavy c++/c coding. There is a lot of problems to solve along the way, like memory issues, synchronization issues, writing script to draw graph for analysis, etc. None of this is extremely difficult to solve with the help of ChatGPT, but they are kind of annoying when they show up over and over again.
