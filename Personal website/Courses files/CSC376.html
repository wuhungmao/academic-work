I took this course with professor Jessica Burgner. This course is more math heavy compare to other cs courses, though there are some programming to do. Each assignment must be completed in a group of two, so finding a good teammate is a great start. My teammate was absolutely amazing. He can make every assignment submitted look perfect and present the work in a highly organized and professional way. I am truly impressed by the amount of effort he put in the assignment. Other than that, I would recommend this course to people who loves linear algebra and programming.
